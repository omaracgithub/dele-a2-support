## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is my progress saved?**
Yes. All progress is stored locally on your device.

**Can I use the app offline?**
Yes. All exam content is bundled with the app. No internet needed.

**How is the exam scored?**
Following official DELE A2 rules: Group 1 (Reading + Writing) must score 30+ and Group 2 (Listening + Speaking) must score 30+ to pass.

**Can I retake exams?**
Yes. You can retake any exam as many times as you want.
