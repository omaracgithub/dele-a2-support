# Privacy Policy — DELE A2 Exam Practice

Last updated: March 22, 2026

## Overview
DELE A2 Exam Practice is an offline exam preparation app. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency.

## Data Collection
This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device only.

## No Account Required
The app does not require registration or sign-in.

## No Analytics or Tracking
We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools of any kind.

## No Third-Party Data Sharing
No data is shared with third parties.

## Local Storage
Exam progress and scores are saved using on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

## Children's Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes
We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page.
